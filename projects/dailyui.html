DAILY UI CHALLENGE

The DailyUI challenge is designed to help develop design skills over a 100 day period. Each day you are sent an email with a indication of what to design for that day. Upon completion the design is submitted to Dribbble and reviewed by the community. The project is ongoing but always being updated.

WHY THE DAILYUI CHALLENGE?

I wanted to use this challenge as a way to extend skills already available to me but also learn new skills along the way. This allows me to develop into a stronger understanding in design principles and working with constructive feedback.

DESIGNING THE PERFECT EMAIL

The design process of the emails involves working closely with a company's marketing team to ensure brand consistency and that the desired message will be delivering the perfect message to the recipient. This includes researching similar brands to understand how they may be solving the same challenge, but also to make sure that the initial designs ensure are competitive.

Examples of campaigns created include Welcome emails, birthday emails, cart abandonment emails and monthly newsletters.

THE DAILY APPROACH

Each day upon receiving a brief I would consider my options, trying to think a little more out of the box to allow me to try and design something I wouldn’t normally do, even pushing myself outside of my comfort zone sometimes.
