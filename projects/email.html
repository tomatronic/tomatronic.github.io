Email design & Development

EMAIL DESIGN AND DEVELOPMENT

Over the last few years I have worked with a variety of brands to design and build emails designed with best practice in mind. A client would require my assistance due to the complex nature of email coding. It is difficult for a client to ensure compatibility of an email across as many email clients as possible due to individual requirements of email clients.

CHALLENGES IN EMAIL

Email clients such as Outlook 2010 use different rendering engines to email clients such as gmail. Because of this what may work in one place may look completely different in another. As an email is another face of a business it is important that an email displays as well as possible to everyone.

DESIGNING THE PERFECT EMAIL

The design process of the emails involves working closely with a company's marketing team to ensure brand consistency and that the desired message will be delivering the perfect message to the recipient. This includes researching similar brands to understand how they may be solving the same challenge, but also to make sure that the initial designs ensure are competitive.

Examples of campaigns created include Welcome emails, birthday emails, cart abandonment emails and monthly newsletters.

CONSISTANT RENDERING

The build process involved ensuring the most up to date industry leading code was implemented to ensure consistent rendering across a huge range of email clients, this meant a continual development of the framework used. Once an email is built it would be rigorously tested within the litmus testing environment to ensure consistent rendering across email clients.
