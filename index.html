ABOUT

WHO AM I?

My name is Thomas Spencer, I currently work in Brighton primarily developing emails and landing pages for a range of high profile clients. For a full overview of my skills and experiences you can find my CV here or a range of examples of my work here. Please do get in touch if you would like to discuss a project!

WHERE HAVE I BEEN?

I graduated from the University of Brighton in 2011 after studying ‘Digital Media Design’, this course really did help bring my creativity to a whole new level. Within the course I learnt how to use the majority of the Adobe suite and completed a range of projects from website design and builds to the production of small films.

I am currently working for a major ESP in Brighton as a Senior Creative Developer. The role entails a range of skills including but not limited to the designing and building of emails, assisting the internal marketing team with a range of print and digital projects and reviewing team processes to ensure we are working as efficiently as possible.

WHAT I USE MOST DAYS

I use sketch for a mixture of projects including wireframing, initial mockups, illustration and asset creation.

For coding I use atom, I find it is really intuative. I will use it in the deveopment of landing pages, email development and website development.

I tend to use photoshop for creating email and website designs that require a little more work with imagery.

The majority of print work I create is within InDesign, this can include brochures, case studies and posters.

Any animation I create tends to be in after effects, I also think that After effects is my favourite of the Adobe suite.
